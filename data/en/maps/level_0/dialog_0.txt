CONTROLS

The game starts in the initialization phase.
During this phase, you can switch the initial position of your characters by selecting them (left click) and clicking on the desired position.
Don't forget to unselect the character (right click) when you're done.
When you are ready to play, just click on any empty square to open the main menu.
From the main menu, select "start" to begin turn 1.
Enjoy the experience.
